THE STORY

15 years of exponential experience in creative design and innovation

Tanushree has an unremitting passion for designing eclectic spaces and inherent talent in conceiving and creating innovative, out of the box and functional layouts. This passion led her to pursue a diploma in Interior Design post graduating from Delhi University. Thereafter, she kicked off a full-time career in interior desiging by working with some of the leading Architecture & Design firms in India.

In the early years of her career, she was in Delhi but later moved to Mumbai with a dream to make it big in the interior design world. Tanushree's 15 years of exponential experience is what makes her one of the influential innovators in the design industry.

2015, was the year when she conceived the idea of Limehouse Design Studio. Her portfolio includes some of the best award-winning projects successfully completed during her earlier stints. In a short span of 4 years, the studio boasts of an impressive line of projects completed, with many happy clients!
